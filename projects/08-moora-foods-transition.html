Context

Moora Foods operated three Subway franchise outlets across Dubai, with over 70 staff and a decade-plus archive of customer data.

I was brought on to oversee the operational handover of the entire franchise portfolio in the lead-up to a full business sale — hybrid and hands-on: consultant, mediator, and implementation lead across staff, store and stakeholder levels.

Stakeholder Map

One person, eight relationships to hold steady at once.

What I Did

  • —Mapped equipment, leases and asset valuation across all three outlets
  • —Built detailed profit and loss breakdowns
  • —Prepared final pricing feasibility for the buyer consortium
  • —Handled sensitive documentation — visas, passports, serial numbers, bank accounts
  • —Aligned both parties on sale terms so trading continued without a single day's disruption
